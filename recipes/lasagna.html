Lasagna

This lasagna is the perfect weeknight recipe for a dinner party because we will be doing majority of our work the day before. When the time comes, only thing you'll have to do will be to assemble it on tray and bake it!

    Ingredients
  • Ground beef
  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spice mix
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Mozzarella cheese
    Steps
  1. Start by cooking ground beef until it has browned to your liking, it should take about 10-15 minutes.
  2. Mix in your choice of spice mix to ground beef and let spices cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add your tomato sauce and simmer for at least an hour. You can water if it starts getting too dry. You have to stir occasionally to prevent it from sticking.
  4. In a different pot, add your butter, after it has melted add your flour and cook it until the rawness of the flour goes away. Mix in your milk and simmer for 15-20 minutes stirring frequently and voila, your bechamel sauce is ready.
  5. In a baking pan, layer pasta, bolognese sauce and bechamel sauces in order until you used all of them.
  6. Bake it covered for 30 minutes and after your pasta is done, top it all of with mozzarella cheese and put it under broiler for approximately 5 minutes or the cheese has been melted to your liking.
